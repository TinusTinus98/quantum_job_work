Software Test Engineer

Qrypt is a post quantum cryptography company building quantum-secure software and hardware in partnership with world-class national laboratories and academic institutions. The foundation of Qrypt technology is published by our experts in peer-reviewed scientific journals and supported by a growing portfolio of international patents. Qrypt’s product portfolio builds on these technologies and assets to ensure enduring data security in the quantum age.

As a Software Test Engineer at Qrypt, it will be your responsibility to ensure that quality software is delivered to our customers. You will play a crucial role in agile software development by gathering requirements, defining the definition of done, and writing and executing acceptance tests. It will be your responsibility to validate new features, providing feedback to the software engineering team throughout the entire software development lifecycle. You will work closely with project managers, software engineers, and other test engineers to assess risk and assist in the deployment of robust software.

Qrypt provides tools to help developers encrypt sensitive data, so that their applications and infrastructure will be secure against attack both today and by future quantum computers.  We work with advanced technologies including quantum random number generation, post-quantum cryptography, secure messaging, and privacy preservation techniques. 

Preferred location is New York City but will consider alternate remote locations: Bay Area-CA, Seattle-WA, Austin-TX, RTP-NC, Boston-MA or other remote

Requirements

  • 2+ years of experience with software quality and performance testing of a web application
  • Ability to write automated tests in Python, JavaScript, and C++
  • Experience with Microsoft Azure Pipelines or other CI/CD tools
  • Ability to analyze and troubleshoot/debug software issues
  • Ability to autonomously execute test plans
  • Strong communication skills. Able to work effectively as part of an agile team
  • Technical degree in Technology, Engineering, or Math

 

Attitude

  • You take pride in the fact that when you say something works, it works
  • You find satisfaction in breaking software
  • You don’t need hand holding. You understand company goals, the part you play in them, and are comfortable taking initiative when it’s for the good of the mission
  • You are a problem solver who can identify root causes and solutions through creative testing
  • You want to be part of a team creating products that impact the world for the better 
  • You're interested in working with advanced technologies based on the quantum information sciences

  • Gym Reimbursement
  • But above all, be part of an amazing team! Together, we’ll make this world a safer place!

 

About Qrypt

Qrypt is an early-stage technology startup building the next generation of cryptography to protect from the threat of quantum computers and those machines and mathematics yet to be invented. Our passion is to help organizations and people keep their data private – forever. We securely build, design, and deploy safe systems that ensure people’s privacy. Together, we will transform the way the world safeguards data and communications!

The worldwide cyber community is undergoing a once-in-a-generation transition to quantum-safe technologies, with Qrypt leading the way. We care most about securing the intellectual property of nations and corporations, and value the privacy of individuals. We are founding members of the national Quantum Economic Development Consortium and the Mid-Atlantic Quantum Alliance. Qrypt participates in global standards bodies for communications security, financial transactions, and critical infrastructure.
