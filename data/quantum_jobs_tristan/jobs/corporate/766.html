Electrical Engineer I/II

Job Description

QuEra Computing Inc. is seeking an Electrical Engineer to design and build Analog and Mixed-Signal circuits critical to the operation of our quantum computer. The Electrical Engineer must be able to work on a wide scope of problems, from debugging embedded systems to simulating analog electronics. The ideal candidate for this position will enjoy working on a small, fast-paced team where independent work and clear communication are equally valued.

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS

  • Proficiency in analog circuit simulation using spice
  • Experience with PCB Design using Altium Designer
  • Experience with key electronic components: voltage regulator, op-amp, ADC, DAC, passive components, MOSFET, BJT, Diode
  • Experience using test equipment, including oscilloscopes and function generators
  • Familiarity with the PCB manufacturing process
  • At least two years of professional experience as an electrical engineer

ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Design analog and mixed-signal printed circuit boards in Altium Designer
  • Design analog and mixed-signal schematics
  • Manage communications with PCB manufacturers
  • Order circuit components and maintain inventory of key hardware
  • Debug and fix printed circuit boards

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS

  • Bachelor or Master in Electrical Engineering
  • Experience with RF Layout considerations
  • Experience with Python, C++, C, Arduino, PIC
  • Experience with VHDL or Verilog
  • Experience with DSP, RF, SDR
