Associate Transformational Physicist/ Transformational Physicist

Requisition ID: R10032840

  • Category: Research and Sciences
  • Location: Linthicum, Maryland, United States of America
  • Citizenship Required: United States Citizenship
  • Clearance Type: SCI
  • Telecommute: No- Teleworking not available for this position
  • Shift: 1st Shift (United States of America)
  • Travel Required: Yes, 10% of the Time
  • Positions Available: 7

Our employees are not only part of history, they're making history.

The Advanced Technology Lab of Northrop Grumman Mission Systems is seeking Physicists. The qualified candidate will become part of Northrop Grumman’s Transformational Technology Business Area. Transformational Technology is focused on developing state-of-the-art next generation superconducting electronics (SCE) and advanced computing technologies. Qualified Physicists will have knowledge and/or experience with some of the following: - superconducting tunnel junction theory - transport phenomena - solid-state qubit operation - microwave engineering - low-temperature experiments - quantum mechanics and quantum computing - RSFQ - superconducting circuit design - measurement of superconducting devices - digital logic design/development - superconducting electronics.

This position requires on site work at our Linthicum, MD Advanced Technologies Lab.

This requisition may be filled at an Associate Transformational Physicist level or a Transformational Physicist level. Qualifications for both are listed below:

Basic Qualifications for an Associate Transformational Physicist:

  • Must have a Bachelors Degree in Physics or Applied Physics with at least 6 months of relevant experience.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a security clearance (US citizenship is required for this)
  • Experience or knowledge in one or more of the following: superconducting tunnel junction theory, transport phenomena, solid-state qubit operation, microwave engineering, low-temperature experiments, quantum mechanics, RSFQ, superconducting circuit design, measurement of superconducting devices, quantum computing, digital logic design/development, or superconducting electronics

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Familiarity with decoherence mechanisms, physics-based simulation, analog and digital electronics, computer simulations, dilution refrigerator operation desired
  • Familiarity with RSFQ, memory, Cadence, mask layout, multilevel circuit design, and design verification desired - Familiarity with superconducting qubits, quantum computing, and classical computer science desired

Basic Qualifications for an Associate Transformational Physicist:

  • Must have a Bachelors Degree in Physics or Applied Physics with at least 2 years of relevant experience; 0 years with a Masters Degree.
  • Ability to obtain and maintain a security clearance (US citizenship is required for this)
  • Experience or knowledge in one or more of the following: superconducting tunnel junction theory, transport phenomena, solid-state qubit operation, microwave engineering, low-temperature experiments, quantum mechanics, RSFQ, superconducting circuit design, measurement of superconducting devices, quantum computing, digital logic design/development, or superconducting electronics

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Familiarity with decoherence mechanisms, physics-based simulation, analog and digital electronics, computer simulations, dilution refrigerator operation desired
  • Familiarity with RSFQ, memory, Cadence, mask layout, multilevel circuit design, and design verification desired - Familiarity with superconducting qubits, quantum computing, and classical computer science desired
