Quantum Sales Executive

Location: Remote

Team: BizDev

Type: Full-Time

IonQ is developing the world's most powerful full-stack quantum computer based on trapped-ion technology. We are pushing past the limits of classical physics and current supercomputing technology to unlock a new era of computing. Quantum computing has the potential to impact every area of human society for the better. IonQ’s computers will soon redefine industries like medicine, materials science, finance, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cryptography, and more. IonQ is at the forefront of this technological revolution.

 

We are looking for a Quantum Sales Executive. As a Quantum Sales Executive, you’ll be part of a cross-functional team whose mission is to lead IonQ on its journey to build the world’s best quantum computers to solve the world’s most complex problems. 

A Quantum Sales Executive is the face to the customer for IonQ, you will be responsible for initiating, maintaining, and growing the customer relationships, This is a worldwide responsibility, so a broad set of relationship and business skills will be needed. The goal of course is the generation of revenue for IonQ, and as such you will be key to the success of IonQ. We are looking for people that are capable and excited to take on this critical role. As a Quantum Sales Executive, you’ll be part of a cross-functional team whose mission is to lead IonQ on its journey to build the world’s best quantum computers to solve the world’s most complex problems.

Responsibilities: 

  • Develop a pipeline of Commercial and Government opportunities for IonQ’s Quantum Computers.
  • Qualify the opportunities based on availability of budget, skills, interest, executive commitment, and timing. 
  • Form proposals for access to IonQ’s system via the cloud, as a remote dedicated system, or an “on-prem” dedicated system. Take into consideration local restrictions and customs. 
  • Negotiate agreements and close business relationships.
  • Be responsible for customer satisfaction, bringing attention to the broader team any inhibitors or concerns related to the relationship. 

You’d be a good fit with: 

  • An undergraduate degree in Engineering, Science, Math, Computer Science or another relevant technical field. 
  • Experience in “Business Development” or “Sales”.

You’d be a great fit with: 

  • Demonstrable Business Experience/Acumen.
  • Demonstrated experience working with Governments, National Labs, or Technology Companies. 
  • Solid negotiating skills.

Our HQ is located in College Park, Maryland and we are a 15 minute metro ride from Washington DC. We are actively building out teams in the Washington D.C., Seattle, and Boston metro areas and will want to locate you in one of these geographies. However, IonQ will be expanding into additional geographies both domestically and internationally so don’t let this stop you from applying.
