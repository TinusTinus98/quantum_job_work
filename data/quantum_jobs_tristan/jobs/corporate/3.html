Senior/Principal Software Developer (Quantum Network Controller)

Aliro is developing foundational technologies in Quantum Networking and Communications. As a senior member of our team you will be contributing to this foundation by developing an SDN-based controller for the new field of quantum networking and communications.  We are looking for someone who is motivated, and excited, by the opportunity to engage in a new field of research and engineering that will be transformative. 

What you’ll do:

  • Leverage software engineering skills and past experiences to develop a quantum networking software controller. 
  • Develop the interfaces to the controller, deployment and update methodologies and ensure the solution is scalable as quantum networks grow in scope and complexity.
  • Be an integral part of defining the next generation of quantum infrastructure and technology as you build the tools to make the quantum network a reality.

Required background:

  • 5+ years experience developing NMS/SDN products for IP network field
  • Leadership experience building a product with a web app/services architecture in a Linux environment. 
  • At least 2 years of professional experience programming in Python.
  • Knowledge of UNIX or Linux
  • Comfortable working in a new field from a ‘blank sheet of paper’.
  • Good communication and collaboration skills
  • Bachelor’s Degree in CS, Math, EE, or a related field

Preferred background:

  • Experience building or integrating with network management/control systems.
  • Network architecture experience in planning or deploying multi-layer networks (routers and optical systems)
  • Experience with SW/Applications in managing/operating/troubleshooting IP networks
  • Familiarity with YANG/NETCONF configuration model and protocol.
  • Familiar with Agile development methodologies
  • Prior experience in an early stage startup environment is highly desired.

Bonus background:

  • Graduate degree in CS, Math, EE, or a related field
  • Quantum computing experience or training, either professionally or as a passion
  • Experience with contemporary open source NMS and/or SDN controller implementations and deployments.

Community Manager

Background

Aliro Quantum is on a mission to build the quantum internet— the next networking revolution that will solve big problems in medicine, cybersecurity, AI, astronomy, and so much more. Spun out of Harvard University and backed by leading investors, Aliro delivers a full quantum network stack to build and operate entanglement-based quantum networks, enabling ultra secure communication, clustered quantum computing, and quantum sensing. It’s a giant undertaking, but our team of quantum experts are up for it. Want to join us? 

The Role

Aliro is seeking a Community Manager to help us build a movement of people who will build the quantum internet— from technologists to government officials to entrepreneurs and beyond. This is a great opportunity to get in early at an established startup with massive potential. The ideal candidate has experience in B2B community relations; you are a connector, communicator, and a doer with a healthy curiosity for deep tech. As a friendly voice of Aliro, you will oversee social media, events, newsletters, partner, and website management. 

Responsibilities

  • Events:  Manage Aliro’s events calendar. Coordinate sponsorships, speaking, attendees, materials, and promotion. Produce Aliro webinars. Attend and form new contacts.
  • Social Media:  Run Aliro’s social media accounts with best practices and energy. Share useful content, respond quickly, and build Aliro’s following.
  • Newsletters:  Launch a monthly newsletter as a key community touch point. Collaborate with the Aliro team to ensure the content is both engaging and technically accurate.
  • Content:  Plan and execute Aliro’s content calendar with lots of team input and contribution. Ensure we publish a continuous drum beat of content reflecting our QN thought leadership.
  • Public Relations:  Work with our PR consultancy to ensure they have the content and updates they need to engage with press and analysts. 
  • Partnerships:  Help forge and nurture partner relationships with other vendors and curators of quantum developer communities. 
  • Learning:  As a key representative of the Aliro brand, you will need to articulate what we do, which is not always easy! We don’t expect you to be a quantum expert, but we are looking for an insatiable learner who will independently research and ask enough questions to be dangerous— staying current with what we do and our competitive positioning. 

Qualifications

  • 3-5 years in demand gen and/or community relations
  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM 
  • Outgoing personality with a large network
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Sharp project management skills
  • Startup experience and familiarity with CMS, CRM
  • Ability to travel 

Benefits

  • Competitive comp plan (salary and equity)
  • Full benefits package 
  • Unlimited PTO 
  • Work remotely
