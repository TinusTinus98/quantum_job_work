Riverlane has experts in mathematics, computer science, chemistry, physics and quantum computing. Together, we are growing fast and need smart people to join our friendly team.

Introduction

Our world-leading, multi-disciplinary team is based in the heart of Cambridge, UK.  We work together to develop pioneering quantum software. Curious by nature, and optimistic in outlook, we have a razor-sharp focus on doing whatever it takes to get to useful quantum computing, sooner.
